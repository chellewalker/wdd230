Target Audience

The target audience for Superplex Golfing primarily fall into two categories; firstly are older teens, who would likely come later in the afternoon after school lets out or at all times during the weekend; the second primary group would be groups of families who would use the facility as a source of family bonding.

Two Personas

Jane Levvite is a 17 year old high school student who enjoys spending time with friends in weekly activities. She is not particulalrly physically active, but is willing to try new things for the experience.

John McLuffin is a 33 year old father of two, 8 and 11, who works fulltime. He likes to bring his wife and children on weekend outings to spend time with them, and prefers to find activities that could broadern his family's horizons.

Five Scenarios

Sara wants to visit Superplex Golfing with her friends in the afternoon. She thinks it's too late to make a reservation and only enters the site to learn how long the location is open for and its street address.

Paul wants to practice golfing over the weekened; we enters the website and navigates to the "Make a Reservation" button to schedule time at 1:00 PM, when the location would likely be less busy.

Luke and Leia want to go on a date and determined that Superlex Golfing could be an interesting place to visit. They check the information page before navigating to the "Make a Reservation" button for Friday night.

Alfredo wants to find out the hours of operation for Superplex Golfing for a family outing over the weekened. He navigates to the website's home page and finds the avilible times at the top of the page.

Hondo is visiting the city where Superplex Golfing is located and wants to look around for a cost-effective activity. He finds the Superplex Golfing website and navigates to its "Rates" page to learn about how much a session would cost.
